Vitor Moreiran

Hey, it’s me! I explore how internet access and technology shape the ways we connect, share, and create.

Growing up in Guaratinguetá, São Paulo, near South America's largest technical-military educational complex and the National Sanctuary in Aparecida do Norte, I became fascinated by how systems and cultures intersect. The internet became my gateway to knowledge and expression that weren’t available to most people around me, shaping how I navigate the world.

With a background in advertising and technology, I’ve worked as a full-time designer in marketing agencies and at web culture events like BRIFW and REBUDIGITAL. My work moves between digital storytelling, creative technology, and 3D design, experimenting with tools like Blender, TouchDesigner, and AI.

I focus on building immersive experiences for brands, collectives, and individuals, exploring how virtual landscapes and interactive media can reshape the way we experience identity and digital spaces.

More than a medium for creation, I see technology as a way to amplify underrepresented voices and reimagine narratives. My projects blend self-discovery with the exploration of digital environments, avatars, and the relationship between tech and culture. Through visuals, interactive experiences, and conversations, I aim to create work that sparks new perspectives and meaningful connections.
